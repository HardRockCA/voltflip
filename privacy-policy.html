Volt Flip - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

Volt Flip is developed by Christopher Liebe. Your privacy is important to me. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

Player Name (optional): If you choose to enter a name for the global leaderboard, that name is stored on our servers along with your score and game mode. This is entirely optional. You can play the full game without entering a name.

Scores: When you submit a score to the global leaderboard, your score and game mode are stored on our servers.

Local Data: The app stores your preferences, local scores, and settings on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your real name, email address, location, device identifiers, or any personal information beyond the optional player name you choose to enter. We do not use analytics or tracking tools. We do not serve ads.

Third-Party Services

Supabase: Global leaderboard data (player names and scores) is stored using Supabase, a cloud database service. Their privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

RevenueCat: In-app purchases are processed through RevenueCat. They may collect purchase-related data as described in their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Apple: Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

Children's Privacy

Volt Flip does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The optional leaderboard name feature does not require any real personal information.

Data Deletion

If you would like your leaderboard data removed, please contact me at the email below and I will delete it promptly.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
