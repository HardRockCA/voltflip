Volt Flip

Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 6, 2026

Volt Flip ("the App") is developed by Christopher Liebe. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

Volt Flip does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

• Your best scores and score history
• Your selected theme preference
• Your Pro Pass purchase status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase ("Pro Pass") processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. All transactions are governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Volt Flip is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
